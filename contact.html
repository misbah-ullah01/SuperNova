Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

+
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Typically, small projects take 2-4 weeks, medium projects 4-8 weeks, and larger campaigns 8-12 weeks or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you work with startups?

+
Absolutely! We work with startups, established businesses, and everything in between. Our Starter package is specifically designed for growing businesses with limited budgets. We believe great creative is for everyone.

What is included in your packages?

+
Each package includes social media content creation, strategy support, and reporting. The number of posts, videos, and services increase with each tier. Check our Services page for detailed breakdowns of each package.

How do you measure success?

+
We track KPIs including engagement rates, reach, conversions, and ROI. Each project starts with defined goals, and we provide regular reporting on progress. Your success metrics guide our strategy.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

+
Yes! We offer flexibility in our packages. You can upgrade or downgrade based on your needs. Just reach out to your account manager to discuss changes.

What industries do you specialize in?

+
We work across diverse industries including fashion, tech, e-commerce, food & beverage, finance, and more. Our diverse team brings expertise across multiple sectors, allowing us to understand unique market dynamics.
